# ATOMOS #0

*a thought from Mark Leidner*

---

*[Italian version below — La versione italiana è qui sotto]*

Ancient philosophers considered atoms the ultimate and indivisible entities of matter and the Universe, and although we now know they are not truly indivisible, that even smaller particles compose these objects, we still use the word *atom* to refer to something singular and no longer divisible.

Welcome.
This is issue 0 of a new section of the newsletter, called **ATOMOS**.

In this section we'll feature
brief quotes,
tiny stories,
microscopic descriptions,
and invisible explanations.
About the world, about time, about history, about the crossing things that pass through us.

Let's begin, one step at a time.

> one does not begin a poem
> one abandons one's life.
>
> — Mark Leidner
