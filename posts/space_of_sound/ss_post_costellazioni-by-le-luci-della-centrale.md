It’s talking about something you recognize, but is nonetheless foreign to you. It’s not yours, but it’s in every place of your memory; you’ve listened to it so much that you feel a part of you is also defined in relation to it. It’s the record you return to, that you leave before departing, that you find again when you return, from which you distance yourself when you distance yourself from everything. It contains everything and all the rest it doesn’t yet contain, it is the haven of what you have lived that makes room for what you have not yet experienced. It contains places where you have already been but which you visit to connect them to this sound map. </p><p><em><strong>Sound constellation</strong></em>.</p><p>The songs within it are stars, which create constellations if you look at them from a specific point - Earth, life: they are not necessarily there to form those symbols, but your perspective gives you those meanings.</p><p>You create them, the artist creates them, they are different, they are the same.</p><div><hr></div><p><strong><a href="https://open.spotify.com/artist/6lnSL91XbQXZJZw60GyyAi?si=5_BY6duJR6KrnGL-2mZtxQ">Le Luci della Centrale Elettrica</a></strong> is <a href="https://open.spotify.com/artist/6SZXiHJt5lhwBQ4OVTGC6S?si=QhaazM7xQbKyiOWA5xc8rg">Vasco Brondi</a>, and it was his project from 2007 to 2019; now he continues to tell stories producing music with his own name, after having abandoned the name of the project with a farewell concert tour - even though every now and then he lights one up in individual concerts, and these small events remain there to form a memory. </p><p><em><strong>Time constellation</strong></em>.</p><p><strong><a href="https://open.spotify.com/album/6NkWD9vHhGV60KdEKAY0Xu?si=8eMtWizdQdWLGuviAlhgwA">Costellazioni</a></strong> was published in 2014 with the label <em>La Tempesta Dischi</em>.</p><iframe class="spotify-wrap album" data-attrs="{&quot;image&quot;:&quot;https://i.scdn.co/image/ab67616d0000b27338c5f6e16545e227c9023629&quot;,&quot;title&quot;:&quot;Costellazioni&quot;,&quot;subtitle&quot;:&quot;Vasco Brondi, Le Luci Della Centrale Elettrica&quot;,&quot;description&quot;:&quot;Album&quot;,&quot;url&quot;:&quot;https://open.spotify.com/album/6NkWD9vHhGV60KdEKAY0Xu&quot;,&quot;belowTheFold&quot;:true,&quot;noScroll&quot;:false}" src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/6NkWD9vHhGV60KdEKAY0Xu" frameborder="0" gesture="media" allowfullscreen="true" allow="encrypted-media" loading="lazy" data-component-name="Spotify2ToDOM"></iframe><div><hr></div><div><hr></div><blockquote><pre><code><code>dammi solo quello che mi disorienta</code></code></pre></blockquote><div id="youtube2-h645t0hOc-Q" class="youtube-wrap" data-attrs="{&quot;videoId&quot;:&quot;h645t0hOc-Q&quot;,&quot;startTime&quot;:null,&quot;endTime&quot;:null}" data-component-name="Youtube2ToDOM"><div class="youtube-inner"><iframe src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/h645t0hOc-Q?rel=0&amp;autoplay=0&amp;showinfo=0&amp;enablejsapi=0" frameborder="0" loading="lazy" gesture="media" allow="autoplay; Apart from this, <em>there is silence tonight (c’è silenzio</em> <em>stasera)</em>. Under a <em>silver </em>sky (cielo<em> </em>d<em>’argento</em>), a sky that covers the night, we have coordinates: a <em>railway</em>, a recently built <em>mosque</em>, and then a <em>church</em>, a <em>castle</em>, a <em>road</em>; we have music that disorients the silence, and that music comes from a machine, or from <em>North Africa</em>, or from <em>Eastern Europe</em>, or from a machine in one of these two places so far away. We are in a place between the Moon and Northern Italy, we might as well be alone with the memory of the earth, Emilia and the Moon elsewhere, in Poland, in <em>Krakow</em>. There are two <em>torn (strappati)</em> hearts, thank goodness they’re not ours. There is a monument that remembers the suffering that men are capable of inflicting on other men. We promise not to tear out our hearts, but to safeguard them.</p><p>It’s a song of geographical references, of places like pins on a map, of memories and relationships and connections like a thread linking those points. It’s a surface without a center, it has no direction, and so we search for a meaning, a direction: we create a new theory of gravity, the first version of this theory is only <em>momentary</em>, for now it’s enough for us.</p><p class="button-wrapper" data-attrs="{&quot;url&quot;:&quot;https://spaceofsound.substack.com/?utm_source=substack&amp;utm_medium=email&amp;utm_content=share&amp;action=share&quot;,&quot;text&quot;:&quot;Share Space of Sound&quot;,&quot;action&quot;:null,&quot;class&quot;:null}" data-component-name="ButtonCreateButton"><a class="button primary" href="https://spaceofsound.substack.com/?utm_source=substack&amp;utm_medium=email&amp;utm_content=share&amp;action=share"><span>Share Space of Sound</span></a></p><p>We are every place we fly to, every place we visit: we let ourselves be disoriented, we <em>only</em> want what <em>disorients</em> us, we are the action of desperation and the thought of hope. We pray, if we do, for all of this, to arrive in <em>Germany</em>, in <em>Turkey</em>, in <em>Greece</em>, driving in shifts and resting in shifts, a chant <em>to doze off (appisolarci)</em>, water to cross our <em>deserts</em> - and how much water is truly needed, by those who traverse real deserts!</p><p>We want the <em>means</em> to lift ourselves above them, to make them sufficient so that we die.</p><blockquote><pre><code><code>felicemente, felicemente, felicemente al di sopra dei loro mezzi</code></code></pre></blockquote><div><hr></div><blockquote><pre><code><code>qui sono le undici, ci sono meno tre gradi, chiamami quando vuoi</code></code></pre></blockquote><div id="youtube2-OuyN-akButU" class="youtube-wrap" data-attrs="{&quot;videoId&quot;:&quot;OuyN-akButU&quot;,&quot;startTime&quot;:null,&quot;endTime&quot;:null}" data-component-name="Youtube2ToDOM"><div class="youtube-inner"><iframe src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/OuyN-akButU?rel=0&amp;autoplay=0&amp;showinfo=0&amp;enablejsapi=0" frameborder="0" loading="lazy" gesture="media" allow="autoplay; fullscreen" allowautoplay="true" allowfullscreen="true" width="728" height="409"></iframe></div></div><p><em><strong>Macbeth nella nebbia</strong></em> is missed waits, eyes that change, conquests from across the Channel. Your eyes were <em>green (verdi)</em>, now after thirty they seem to welcome <em>fires</em>, trees and flames, Venus and Mars - will they be red? None of this, <em>nothing more depends on the alignment of the planets</em>. All the attentions, pressures, aspirations, objectives, you distance yourself from them in flight, with <em>transparent wings</em>, with <em>strong winds</em> toward England, the night is inside you, your England too, what you defeat outside of you you defeat first <em>in a small part inside you</em>, the <em>dirty air</em> is like your thoughts, clouding your sight is a prerogative of the mind.</p><p>Now everyone has aged, the musicians, your eyes, yourself, time, odd, <em>in</em> <em>four-four time, postmodern</em>. Macbeth cannot save himself, you cannot either, the night arrives, your <em>inner fathers and mothers</em> are not the three witches. So don’t wait for the night, fly, keep flying: nothing <em>can reach you</em>, beyond the fog and dirty air you will find that</p><blockquote><pre><code><code>il futuro era sempre lì a sorriderci</code></code></pre></blockquote><div><hr></div><blockquote><pre><code><code>l’interpretazione dei sogni che non riesco a ricordarmi</code></code></pre></blockquote><div id="youtube2-r2jkLseUxyE" class="youtube-wrap" data-attrs="{&quot;videoId&quot;:&quot;r2jkLseUxyE&quot;,&quot;startTime&quot;:null,&quot;endTime&quot;:null}" data-component-name="Youtube2ToDOM"><div class="youtube-inner"><iframe src="https://www.youtube-nocookie.com/embed/r2jkLseUxyE?rel=0&amp;autoplay=0&amp;showinfo=0&amp;enablejsapi=0" frameborder="0" loading="lazy" gesture="media" allow="autoplay; fullscreen" allowautoplay="true" allowfullscreen="true" width="728" height="409"></iframe></div></div><p><em><strong>Le ragazze stanno bene</strong></em> is the entire life, the future and everything that will happen. The girls feel great now, they have been vulnerable, they have made mistakes, waited, accepted themselves and their weak points, they have fallen, they have burned everything, <em>setting the sky on fire like a meteorite</em>. They wait for each other at the <em>station</em>, they wait for each other in the <em>car</em>, on trips that are too long, in the white spaces left for the figure of the other, for the missing pieces that the other will have to find and use to build the future together. Without knowing it, the future, having forgotten it, as in a postwar period, being able to rebuild it without instructions of any kind.</p><p>Their home is a collection of noises and voices, and going outside the <em>fire escape</em> is an <em>astronomical observatory</em>, they see everything, everything might not have a meaning, nothing could have a meaning, the meaning of dreams is you, when I don’t find one, they tell each other this. At night we are all tired, but Chiara, but Sara, I am even more beautiful in the evening, <em>when you are very tired</em>.</p><p>This song is perfect, everything is perfect, there is nothing
