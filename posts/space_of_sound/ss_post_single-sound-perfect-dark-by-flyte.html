Eyes almost closing

Flyte is a project from London created in 2013 by Will Taylor and Nicolas Hill , that featured for a while Jon Supran and Sam Barrage .

The style of the band could defined as “chamber-folk”, and the songs are gentle brushes of warm voices and delicate sounds.

Perfect Dark - New York version was published in 2024 under Nettwerk Music Group , and makes no exception. Another version is track #5 of the self-titled album from 2023.

There’s someone under her spell (but who is she ?), there’s a red room and a low moon entering that very room.

It is quite possible that we will fall under that spell, because “ eyes ” are “ almost closing ”, and there’s not much time.

The chorus displays the choice to resist to “ her perfect dark ”, where everywhere else there’s a blackout, and only the moon casts its light into that room.

And maybe her trap is simply love, a kind of love that tangles sheets, and makes you forget everything else; then, sirens will woke us up from our dreams.

The song is a gentle and warm lullaby, with a piano and a delicate drum line which blends everything together, along with the guitar picking.

A wonderful song to play on serene nights.
