A life of love is a long time

The Growlers is a project from Orange County, California. It now comprises one of its founders, singer Brooks Nielsen, and Kyle Straka on keyboard and guitars.

When You Were Made is a song on City Club (2016), the seventh album from the band, published by Cult Records .

The song is a psychedelic ballad on two people who are “not in love anymore”.

It might be because they're afraid, stuck by worries. But Nielsen reassures, with her scratchy voice, that " a life of love is a long time ".

And this happens because you have only one life, so you need to make the world " less cruel ", and not get too down.

The song is a perfect hit for driving-at-sunset-after-you've-broken-up-and-still-feel-something, something strange that still hits you.

After all, we're in California, a cinematic representation of love never hurts. Especially with a bass line and electric guitar like that.
