Can’t Keep Saying Goodbye is a single written by Spencer Ian Rakela of Animals in the Attic , a band from Sacramento, and produced with the contribution of Madi Sipes & The Painted Blue , from San Francisco, and Dent May , from Los Angeles.

An entirely Californian creation, which delivers that sound right from the beginning.

It's a song in which one can't manage to say goodbye: perhaps because we return to where we felt good, perhaps because we still can't tell you " I hate you " after having said " I love you ".

![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/f42db343-caca-4851-b731-c323a5d33b23_1280x720.jpeg)

Animals in the Attic

The sound is a fresh - not icy - wave from the Californian Pacific, and the voices involved are all warm, well balanced: they immediately make you want to hear them live.

Dent May

Despite seeming like a not very layered song, in reality beyond the standard equipment of electric guitars, synth, drums and drum pedals there are also voices used in varied ways, especially in the pre-choruses.

And the outro is worth the price - especially live!
