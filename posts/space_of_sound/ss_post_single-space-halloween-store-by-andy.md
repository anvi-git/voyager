He has a gentle voice, a good demeanour and seems to be someone you could relate to. </p><iframe class="spotify-wrap" src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/track/67S0pUeWJxTlUqsGZIN4vo" frameborder="0" gesture="media" allowfullscreen="true" allow="encrypted-media"></iframe><p>Not to confirm the stereotype, but Andy Shauf comes from Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada. His first published record is from 2006, and he was born in 1987. </p><p>The thing is, he’s a really good songwriter, so he’s maybe more a complex character that appears.</p><p class="button-wrapper"><a class="button primary" href="https://spaceofsound.substack.com/p/single-space-halloween-store-by-andy?utm_content=share&action=share"><span>Share</span></a></p><p><a href="https://open.spotify.com/track/3oWGBitSdsizfiIlCfwUqV?si=03097754d95b4eff">Halloween Store</a> is track #7 on <a href="https://open.spotify.com/album/0TaN6TZg6BpT0lQNJAhGrC?si=G-DcyCKcRJugo_lqPoNT6A">Norm</a>, the 9th studio album by the Canadian songwriter. </p><p>The song is a song that could fit in a short movie. It tells the story of Norm, but he’s not actually narrating anything.
