<p></p><p>I don’t really know which part of a song I prefer, choosing between the lyrics and the rhythm. </p><p>In the case of <strong><a href="http://www.beachhousebaltimore.com/depression-cherry-lyrics/">Space Song</a></strong>, by <strong><a href="http://www.beachhousebaltimore.com/">Beach House</a></strong>, I feel like I don’t have to choose: the song is wholly built with synths and drums as walls, and these walls are connected by <strong>Victoria Legrand</strong>, half of Beach House along with <strong>Alex Scally</strong>.</p><p>The song is contained in the fifth album, <em>Depression Cherry</em>, published in 2015. </p><div class="captioned-image-container"><figure><a class="image-link image2 is-viewable-img" target="_blank" href="https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F41a7d79b-a0d1-499a-a11f-d00d1be52546_1280x1280.webp"><div class="image2-inset"><picture><source type="image/webp" srcset="https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F41a7d79b-a0d1-499a-a11f-d00d1be52546_1280x1280.webp 424w, https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F41a7d79b-a0d1-499a-a11f-d00d1be52546_1280x1280.webp 848w, https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F41a7d79b-a0d1-499a-a11f-d00d1be52546_1280x1280.webp 1272w, https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F41a7d79b-a0d1-499a-a11f-d00d1be52546_1280x1280.webp 1456w" sizes="100vw"><img src="https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F41a7d79b-a0d1-499a-a11f-d00d1be52546_1280x1280.webp" alt="Image"></picture></a></figure></div><p><em>Tender is the night</em>, Legrand sings, but it’s a <em>late night</em>.
