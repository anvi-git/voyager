We’ll never be the same again
Marius Lauber is a young artist from Viersen, Germany; under the moniker Roosevelt he has published in 2018 Young Romance, the second LP, and the one Yr Love comes out from.
The song comes out from a record that presents as a Young Romance form the title, but the result is not a simple crafter song to shout at concerts; yes, the lyrics are not exactly layered, but the sound is solid and has diverse influences.
![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/b0e60293-6bd5-4546-868a-0f8131d5807f_1200x800.jpeg)
There’s electronic music and the drum machine kicks in throughout the song; the end has some synth-y sprouts in it, and the guitars are rock solid.
![](https://substack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com/public/images/8857230f-ecbb-45d5-8878-614d732e74a6_640x640.jpeg)
The loop of the song is hypnotic, and Lauber’s voice is convincing enough - you would probably recognize this on the radio.
A good catchy song - is this not enough?
