The last LP published by <strong><a href="http://www.bigthief.net">Big Thief</a></strong> is an ode to the cosmic infiniteness of space and time. There is love, death, grief, jealousy, but ultimately we all are pervaded by peace and calmness.</p><p>Stillness peaks, <em>Change </em>fades away, and we’ve only played the first Escaping</em>, where <em>“each dimension breaks in two” </em>and <em>“only the winter wind survives”</em>. So let’s follow her voice through <em>Spud Infinity</em>, where she plans to <em>free the celestial body, </em>and learns that the past is not linear, life is a cycle, life has a cycle: drums surround her voice as we arrive at <em>Certainty</em>, where among other things we listen to <em>sine wave is a particle ride, </em>and the guitar is lavishingly laying on a plane next to us.</p><p>The track that shares the same name as the LP is a prayer, a spell, a love letter, a farewell to be read where the<em> dust goes dancing</em>. It’s a song of brush guitars, bells, icicles, and it’s beautiful on a silent night as on a bright morning.</p><p><em>Sparrow </em>is a sweet lullaby held by gentle drums and Lenker’s voice.</p><p>I love the complexity of <em>Little Things</em>, I love <em>how you say when you say what you do</em>, when you’re a <em>little strung out</em>. I could go on by quoting this song, you’d better listen to it (the song I recommend if you only have time for one of these). Claps are a fun touch.</p><p><em>Heavy Bend</em> seemed strange, then I learned it’s the combination of shaker, nylon string guitar and synthesizer. <em>Flower of Blood</em> continues that new sound, <em>blooming on my tongue</em>. <em>Blurred View</em> plays exactly how the title suggests: you have to wipe the windshield to listen to it properly; <em>Red Moon</em> seems to be reddened by the fire camp you’re playing this song around, and it feels exactly like that. <em>Dried Roses</em> starts with a surprisingly good health recommendation, and the fiddle plays harmoniously with Lenker’s voice.</p><p>There’s no reason to skip <em>No Reason</em>, where the sound is as complete as the lyrics, the flute and the tambourine propagate through the air <em>like a fallen eyelash</em>. The drum machine in <em>Wake Me up to Drive</em> would make me doze off, as this song is indeed powerful in lulling you, just like <em>Promise is a Pendulum</em> continues to do, although it can <em>never make the earth turn</em>. <em>12000 Lines</em> are those I hope not to have to read while <em>waiting for my woman to call</em>, and <em>Simulation Swarm</em> is another great track made complete by multiple guitars, drums, and a shaker that matches Lenker's voice ups and downs - although it’s the electric guitar that sticks to you. In <em>Love Love Love</em> starts with a glitch, and then proceeds to ask us to <em>release </em>[her] love. <em>The Only Place</em> has <em>so much to share</em>, but so little to sustain itself: we end up listening to the lyrics, and we learn that <em>when all material scatters</em> <em>/</em> <em>the only place that matters</em> <em>/ is by your side</em>. </p><p><em>Blue Lightning</em> closes the LP with a wish <em>to be</em> [a] <em>mountain and kiss the sky</em>. </p><p>That is sound advice, if you ask me, because</p><blockquote><p><em><strong>I wanna live forever ‘til I die</strong></em></p></blockquote><div><hr></div><iframe class="spotify-wrap album" data-attrs="{&quot;image&quot;:&quot;https://i.scdn.co/image/ab67616d0000b2739ec66d199c19d227f5c37a68&quot;,&quot;title&quot;:&quot;Dragon New Warm Mountain I Believe In You&quot;,&quot;subtitle&quot;:&quot;Big Thief&quot;,&quot;description&quot;:&quot;Album&quot;,&quot;url&quot;:&quot;https://open.spotify.com/album/7Ln81p86r5cCsesd3KBWIY&quot;,&quot;belowTheFold&quot;:true,&quot;noScroll&quot;:false}" src="https://open.spotify.com/embed/album/7Ln81p86r5cCsesd3KBWIY" frameborder="0" gesture="media" allowfullscreen="true" allow="encrypted-media" loading="lazy" data-component-name="Spotify2ToDOM"></iframe><div><hr></div><p>If you liked this, you could share this project with others.
