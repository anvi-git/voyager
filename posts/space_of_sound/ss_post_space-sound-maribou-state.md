<p>I don’t exactly like electronic music. That is probably not true, but I like it blended with some other genre.</p><p>I don’t really know where I’m going with this, so I’ll go straight to the point.</p><div><hr></div><p>I stumbled upon <strong><a href="https://mariboustate.bandcamp.com/">Maribou State</a></strong> through their first studio album, <em>Portraits (2015)</em>. </p><p>I saved one song in my Spotify favourite songs, then lost track of this duo.</p><p>I caught myself listening in loop their second LP, <em>Kingdoms in Colour (2018)</em>, this fall, but I did not connect the dots that those two LPs came from the same group.</p><p>Last January, <strong>Chris Davids</strong> and <strong>Liam Ivorym</strong>, the two English musicists behind Maribou State released <em><a
