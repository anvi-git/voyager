beautiful.</p><p>They formed in 2010 and recently released an EP registered at the Middle Farm Studios in Dartmoor, <strong><a href="https://youtu.be/qkbbI13GXgU">Middle Farm someone replies “<em>wait another year”, </em>and we’re just happy to be interrogated by her voice.</p><p><em>Party </em>is not a song you could party with, but could be the perfect score for an end-of-relationship-kind-of-conversation, making that night “<em>the worst night of </em>[your]<em> life”.</em></p><p><em>To Rage</em> is the perfect sequel to Party, where we’re left tracking the pieces left by those exhausting conversations that explode, when someone storms out of the room and you end up “<em>distraught”</em>.</p><p><em>Be on your way</em> is the perfect therapy session you need after that, when you make sense of what happened, of the planets that mark the distance between you two; and as long as you try to “<em>be on your way”</em>, you’ll find it difficult to “<em>reconstruct the scene</em>”. </p><p>Dandelion exits from that loop, and the music of Haefeli and Aguilella follows that more than ever, lifting a bit up the rhythm: and it’s not “<em>a last song is <em>Neptune</em>, where we come back almost full circle to the Tonra of <strong>If You Leave </strong>(2013), but Tonra here seems to be a bit more liberated to experiment with her voice.
